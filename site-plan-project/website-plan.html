Innovative Construction and Remodeling Services

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: InnovativeConstruction.com

Reason: This name reflects the focus on innovative construction and remodeling services, making it easy for potential clients to find and recognize the company.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide information about durable concrete houses, remodeling services, welding, and zinc roofing. It will serve as a hub for potential clients to learn about the services offered, view past projects, and contact the company.

Scenarios

  • What are the benefits of choosing concrete houses over traditional construction methods?
  • How can I contact the company to get a quote for my remodeling project?

Color Schema

Main Colors: Blue and White

  • Blue: Used for headings and accents (e.g., #1E90FF)
  • White: Used for background and main text (e.g., #FFFFFF)

Typography

Fonts:

  • Headings: Arial, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Times New Roman, serif
